~Help and FAQs~


Below are a some Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) and Tips that will help you utilize our system with maximum efficiency:


~ How To Borrow A Articles & Books ? ~


  • Log In or Register.
  • View the large number of Articles & Bookss available on our system.
  • Select the Articles & Books that you desire to read.
  • Fill in the appropriate borrowing information.
  • Enjoy your reading!

~ How To Register as a Librarian Under the Library System ? ~


  • Gather the necessary academic records and ensure that they're official.
  • Write a brief CV.
  • Gather the information any previous working experiences.
  • Read all the information, to ensure it is correct.
  • Send us an email with all the info HERE.
  • We will respond to your email as soon as possible, thank you for choosing to work with us.

~ How to Reset my Password ? ~


  • Navigate to the login page for your specified module (manager, librarian or user).
  • Click on "Forgot Password".
  • Answer the Forget Password Question correctly.
  • Enter and Confirm your New Password.
  • Your Password has now been successfully changed!

~ How to Return A Borrowed Articles & Books ? ~


  • Login to your Librarian or User Module.
  • Navigate to the View Borrowed Articles & Bookss.
  • Enter the Borrow ID of the borrowed Articles & Books you would like to return.
  • Click on the "Return Articles & Books" Button.
  • Your borrowed Articles & Books has successfully been returned!
  • P.S: You will be fined $5 for every extra day you do not return a borrowed Articles & Books if the due date has passed.

~ How to Print A Table ? ~


  • Login to your specified module (manager, librarian or user).
  • Navigate to any of the tables, for example View Borrowed Articles & Bookss.
  • Click on the "Print Table" button.
  • Select your printer and configure the settings till you have the desired output.
  • Click Print.
